Mott MacDonald

As a UK well-known, world-class planning, construction and operation management company for airports and public construction, it has successful examples all over the world, from Heathrow Terminal 5 in the UK, Department of Environmental Affairs in South Africa to Delhi Metro in India. Jendong hopes to improve the quality of civil airports and emergency heliports to the world-class level through the introduction of Mott MacDonald to Taiwan.

Target of collaboration:

• Civil airports
• Emergency rescue heliports

www.mottmac.com

CIMC

CIMC is the world’s largest designer and manufacturer for passenger boarding bridge. Relevant successful examples include Shanghai Pudong Airport, Amsterdam Airport in the Netherland and Charles de Gaulle Airport in France. Jendong wishes to optimize the passenger boarding bridges in Taiwan and bring them to the world-class level through the cooperation with CIMC.

Target of collaboration:

• Passenger boarding bridges
• Air cargo handling systems
• Baggage handling systems

www.cimc.com/business/airport/

AVIC

AVIC is a designer and manufacturer of hydraulic transparent glass boarding bridge. It also uses the Head of State level bulletproof materials to make boarding bridges. We hope that though our cooperation with AVIC, we can bring the passenger boarding bridges in Taiwan’s airports to another level.

Target of collaboration:

• Passenger boarding bridges

www.avic.com
